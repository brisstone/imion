INTERNATIONAL MARITIME

INSTITUTE OF NIGERIA

(IMION) is established as a think-tank research and policy advocacy institute that would complement government agencies involved in the maritime sector by mobilising maritime experts to assist and support in maritime policy planning and implementation through free exchange of ideas on maritime-related matters.

Join Us Now!

Research and Benchmarking.

It’s a valuable tool for evaluating performance, identifying best practices and uncovering opportunities for improvement.

Collaboration and Partnerships.

To create mechanisms for working together with others in the maritime ecosystem to achieve a common goal.

Capacity Building.

An improvement in the maritime policy landscape towards building collective abilities to be more productive and perform better.

Knowledge Management.

Aimed at capturing, sharing and promoting the effective usage of maritime hindsight, insights and foresight in a coordinated and productive manner.

Engagement with Industry & Stakeholders.

Aimed at knowing who the stakeholders are, towards understanding their interests.

Policy Advocacy $ Implementation.

To create a mechanism towards engaging in social decision-making processes or policy proposals in the maritime environment.
